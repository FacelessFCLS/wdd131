Desert Dungeon

Website planning document

Site Name

Desert Dungeon

This name reflects the focus toward the beloved TTRPG Dungeons & Dragons, along with alluding to the fact that my home of Tucson, Az is a desert.

Otional Domain: desertdungeon.com

Site Purpose

This website is intended to:

  • Showcase a variety of enemies to utilize in combat encounters. (Javascript)
  • Include randomizer programs for rolling a variety of dice and dice amounts.
  • Have a dedicated page that will have a built in "Encounter Balancer" for combat.
  • Have a small sample of a video game to play.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. How accurate is the Encounter Balancer?
  2. Can I roll different dice for different values at the same time?
